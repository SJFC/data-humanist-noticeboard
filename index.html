You Are Not a Bar Chart

A companion to the data humanist noticeboard installation.
An MDes inquiry into how we represent lives in data.

Data humanism looks beyond the graphs and charts of big data to the individual human stories that sit behind them. It asks: what is lost when a life becomes a number? And what might we gain by representing ourselves differently?
